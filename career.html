Military Timeline

October 2019 marked my ten year enlistment in the United States Air Force, with it also marked my return to Lackland AFB, which I hadn't been to since I left basic. Its hard to believe that I'm the same person that first left Lackland to be stationed out in Minot, North Dakota, but I am. In the time I've spent around the world I have had the privilege to have followed, lead, and worked with some of the best troops the Air Force has to offer. I have had the opportunity to meet some of the most capable leaders, work under them, and learn from them. In all of this experience I have gained so much, but unfortunately have also had great losses. And so sometimes you have to go back to where your road started, to learn from the drawing board a bit more. Where do I go from here? I've asked myself; I can only hope and pray that the next years are filled with more happiness and learning.

“None of us are immune from life’s tragic moments. Like the small rubber boat we had in basic SEAL training, it takes a team of good people to get you to your destination in life. You cannot paddle the boat alone. Find someone to share your life with. Make as many friends as possible, and never forget that your success depends on others.” ― William H. McRaven, Make Your Bed: Little Things That Can Change Your Life...And Maybe the World
