Mariana "Dora" Hernandez-Chavez, TSgt, USAF

Section Chief, Personal Property and Passanger Travel

Minot AFB, ND
Technical Sergeant Hernandez-Chavez currently serves as Section Chief, Personal Property, and Passenger Travel at the 5th Logistics Readiness Squadron, Minot Air Force Base, North Dakota. Sergeant Hernandez-Chavez was born in Rio Verde, San Luis Potosi, Mexico, and graduated from Chester W. Nimitz High School in Houston, TX in 2009. She migrated to the United States on October 1996, and became a US Permanente Resident February 2006. She currently holds a Community College of the Air Force degree in Transportation and is currently working on completion of her bachelorâ€™s degree in Applied Science with a specialty in Transportation. Sergeant Hernandez-Chavez is the first and only member of her family currently serving in the Arm forces. She enlisted in the United States Air Force on October 2009 and arrived at Lackland AFB, TX for basic military training and follow-on technical training. She graduated as a Traffic Management Apprentice from the 345th Training Squadron in March 2009. Her first assignment was to the 5th Logistics Readiness Squadron, Minot Air Force Base, ND mastering her technical proficiency and earning her 5-skill level. She has held several positions throughout the spectrum of her career. Her duty stations include Kunsan AB, ROK, Lajes Field, Azores, PT, Hurlburt Field, FL, and Kadena AB, Okinawa, JP. She returned to San Antonio, TX in 2019, beginning her current tour of duty. During her career, she worked at the only Government Owned Government Operated Vehicle Processing Center, located in Lajes Field. She was also Air Force Special Operation Command, Traffic Management Non-Commission Officer of the year for 2017.
